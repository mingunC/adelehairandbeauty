Business Policies

Online Booking System Regulations

Clients are welcome to independently modify or cancel their appointments up to 48 hours before the scheduled time through our online booking system.

For any changes required within the 48-hour window, please contact the salon directly by phone.

Late Arrival Policy

Clients who arrive within 15 minutes of their scheduled appointment time will still be accommodated. However, beyond this 15-minute grace period, we may be unable to provide the scheduled service, and rescheduling or a no-show status may apply.

No-Show Policy

To ensure optimal customer service and effective time management, clients who miss their appointment three times will be subject to having future bookings declined. A cancellation fee will be charged for any no-show appointment. This policy is in place to maintain the quality and efficiency of our service.

Service Agreement

We kindly ask all clients to review and sign our Service Agreement for certain services. This agreement outlines will be sent to your emails about our policies including service details and client responsibilities. Please take a moment sign it before your visit.
